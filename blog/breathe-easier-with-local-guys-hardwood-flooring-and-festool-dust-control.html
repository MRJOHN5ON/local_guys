Breathe Easier with Local Guys Hardwood Flooring and Festool Dust Control 💨

At Local Guys Hardwood Flooring, our dedication to quality extends beyond a beautiful finish. We understand that sanding and refinishing hardwood floors can be a dusty process, which is why we’ve invested in the industry’s leading dust control technology: the Festool CLEANTEC dust collection system.

This two-part system combines powerful, source-specific dust extraction with whole-room air purification, ensuring a cleaner, healthier, and more professional experience for our Longview, Oregon customers.

The Festool CLEANTEC System: A Two-Part Approach to Dust Control

Our primary defense against dust is the Festool CLEANTEC CT series dust extractor. These aren’t just ordinary shop vacs; they are professional-grade dust extractors that connect directly to our sanders and other tools. This allows us to capture up to 99% of dust particles at the source.

* CT HEPA Filters: Every Festool CT extractor we use, such as the popular CT MIDI and CT 36, is equipped with a certified HEPA filter. This means it traps 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, including the fine, harmful dust that can linger in the air long after a job is done.

* Automatic Operation: The CLEANTEC system automatically turns on and off with our power tools, ensuring dust is captured the moment it's created. The anti-static hoses prevent dust from clinging and clogging, maintaining consistent suction throughout the entire project.

The Final Line of Defense: Festool SYS-AIR Air Purifiers

While source extraction is incredibly effective, some microscopic airborne dust is inevitable. To combat this, we deploy the Festool SYS-AIR air purifier in the work area. This portable unit filters the air, capturing any remaining fine dust particles that have escaped the main extractor.

The SYS-AIR can circulate air at a powerful rate of up to 423 cubic feet per minute (CFM), ensuring that your home’s air is scrubbed clean while we work. The result is a dramatic reduction in dust that settles on surfaces and in surrounding rooms. A clean worksite means less time spent on post-project cleanup and a healthier environment for your family.

A Cleaner, Healthier, and More Flawless Finish

Using this integrated Festool system provides tangible benefits you can see and feel. Our process leaves your home cleaner during and after the project, minimizing cleanup. The reduction in airborne dust creates a healthier indoor environment, which is especially important for those with allergies or respiratory sensitivities.

Furthermore, a dust-free environment is critical for achieving a flawless finish on your hardwood floors. Any particles that settle on a freshly applied coat of finish can ruin the final product. By meticulously controlling dust from start to finish, we guarantee a smoother, more durable, and more beautiful result.

When you choose Local Guys Hardwood Flooring, you’re choosing a company that values craftsmanship, professionalism, and your well-being. Our investment in the Festool CLEANTEC and SYS-AIR systems demonstrates our commitment to delivering a premium product with a minimal impact on your home.
