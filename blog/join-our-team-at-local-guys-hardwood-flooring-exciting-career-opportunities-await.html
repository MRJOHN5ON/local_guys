Join Our Team at Local Guys Hardwood Flooring: Exciting Career Opportunities Await

Are you passionate about hardwood flooring and eager to build a rewarding career? Look no further! Local Guys Hardwood Flooring is expanding, and we are excited to invite skilled professionals to join our dedicated team. With a focus on delivering exceptional hardwood flooring services, we want you to be part of our journey to create beautiful spaces.

At Local Guys Hardwood Flooring, we recognize that a fulfilling career is just as important as the beautiful environments we create. We offer competitive pay, outstanding benefits, and a work atmosphere that values hard work and commitment. If you're ready to elevate your skills and make a significant impact, keep reading to discover more about this opportunity!

Our team takes pride in our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. When you join Local Guys Hardwood Flooring, you become part of a family that values its members and promotes growth and development.

We offer a starting wage between $25 and $30 per hour, based on your experience. This competitive salary reflects our appreciation for the unique skills you bring to our team. Additionally, those with advanced skills or certifications have the potential to earn even more, making this a lucrative career choice.

Alongside great pay, we provide Paid Time Off (PTO) to help you maintain a healthy work-life balance. Our benefits package includes a Simple IRA retirement plan with a 3% match. For example, if you contribute $100 per month, we will match $3 each month, helping you build savings for the future while you focus on your work today.

As a team member at Local Guys Hardwood Flooring, you will play a vital role in ensuring our clients receive top-notch flooring service. Your responsibilities will include:

You will accurately measure and cut materials to ensure perfect installation. For instance, knowing how to measure for a 300-square-foot room can help you minimize waste and keep costs down. Additionally, using various tools and machines provided by the company, you will meticulously install hardwood floors, ensuring they are aligned and expertly finished.

Refinishing existing hardwood floors is another essential task. This includes sanding, staining, and applying finishes that enhance the wood grain and protect against wear and tear. Familiarity with sanding machines can improve efficiency and results for larger projects, such as refinishing a 1,500-square-foot space.

Engage directly with clients, addressing their questions and providing guidance on flooring options. Exceptional customer service is a core value at Local Guys Hardwood Flooring. Building rapport with clients fosters trust and contributes to repeat business, which is essential for your success and our continued growth.

Keeping tools and equipment in excellent condition is crucial. You will be responsible for regular maintenance of machines to ensure safety and efficiency. This not only prolongs the life of the tools but also reduces downtime, which can save the company money and keep projects on schedule.

Collaboration is vital. You will work closely with fellow team members, ensuring that all projects are completed not only on time but also meet our quality standards. A strong team environment allows everyone to succeed and contributes to personal growth.

We seek candidates with a solid background in flooring, especially hardwood flooring. Experience in installation and refinishing will give you an edge in the hiring process, as we value expertise in our team.

This job involves physical labor, so candidates must be capable of lifting heavy materials and working in different environments. Daily tasks may require lifting up to 50 pounds or more, so being physically ready is important.

As part of our team, you will travel to various job sites. Having reliable transportation ensures timely arrivals and project completions, which is essential for our operations and client satisfaction.

If you possess the necessary skills and are ready to join a company that values its employees, we want to hear from you! Please click the "Contact" tab on this webpage to submit your resume highlighting relevant experience. Our team will carefully review applications and reach out to qualified candidates for interviews.

Join us at Local Guys Hardwood Flooring and embrace a fulfilling career where your skills and hard work are recognized and rewarded.

Local Guys Hardwood Flooring offers an exciting career path for those skilled in hardwood flooring installation and refinishing. With a great pay structure, comprehensive benefits, and a supportive work environment, we believe that working with us is not just a job; it’s a chance for personal and professional growth.

We encourage you to apply now and take the first step toward a rewarding career with Local Guys Hardwood Flooring. Join our team and play a crucial role in transforming spaces with beautiful hardwood floors!
