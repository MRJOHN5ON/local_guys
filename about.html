Local Guys Hardwood Flooring

Your trusted partner for premium hardwood flooring solutions. With over 25 years of experience as a second-generation contractor, we bring unmatched craftsmanship and expertise to every project. Established in 2017, we've proudly served our community.

Local Guys Hardwood was the winner in the Custom Flooring category of the 2023 Best of SW Washington Awards.

Meet Ben

Ben is a second-generation hardwood flooring specialist. He mentored under his father, Scott Hadlock, and began his foundation at home. At the young age of 13, Ben was invited to travel with his father to install wood floors in cabins in Alaska — a great learning experience for the trade and a bonding experience with his father.

Now, Ben has his own hardwood flooring company and has been managing it since 2017. Local Guys Hardwood is a great way to utilize the skills he learned from his father while combining cutting edge technology. The hardwood flooring industry is a growing one and Ben considers himself a student, always in class learning.
